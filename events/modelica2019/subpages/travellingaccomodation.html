Travelling and Accomodation

The 13th International Modelica Conference takes place March 4th - 6th, 2019 at Ostbayerische Technische Hochschule (University of Applied Sciences) Regensburg, Germany.
 
Venue Address: Seybothstraße 2, 93049 Regensburg, Germany
 
For your convenience, this information is provided as pdf.

Arriving by train

 From the main station, the conference venue is about 1.4 km / 18 minutes to walk.
Please use Deutsche Bahn to search for connections, also for public transport between the airport and Regensburg main station.
 

Arriving by car

  In Regensburg, highway A93 (from Munich via A9) crosses highway A3 (Düsseldorf - Köln - Frankfurt/Main - Nürnberg - Regensburg - Passau).
Please note that there are construction works at the highway A3 in the region of Regensburg. Most of them are planned to be finished until end of 2018, but at least one construction site (from Regensburg University direction Passau) will remain, and traffic jams may occur.
 

Venue map

There is large parking area located between Galgenbergstraße (East), Universtätsstraße (West),
OTH Regensburg (North) and University Regensburg (South). Entry to the parking area both from
Galgenbergstraße and Universtätsstraße. From the parking area, you go north to building A, and turn left over the bridge.

Walking from the main station up Galgenbergstraße, you go right then left in front of a small lake.
You enter the conference area crossing a bridge over the small lake.

Here you will find the conference desk at part of the exhibition. All lecture halls are in area S.
Coffee breaks and lunch are organized at the mensa, area Q.
The poster session will take place in the forum of building "K".


Lecture Halls


 The lecture halls are in the light green area:
  • S051 on floor 0 (300 seats)
  • S052 on floor 0 (130 seats)
  • S053 on floor -1 (145 seats)
  • S054 on floor 1  (690 seats): plenary sessions
  • S057 on floor 0 right hand side from the other lecture halls
  • S157 on floor 1 right hand side from the other lecture halls
  • S159 on floor 1 right hand side from the other lecture halls

 

Climate and Weather

Regensburg straddles the humid continental and oceanic climate zones.
Temperature in March can be expected between 0°C and 8°C.
 

Accomodation

We have preserved a contingent of rooms at Tourismus Regensburg which can be booked directly at this link.
Please note that availability of rooms is guaranteed only until end of 2018.
Besides the official contingent, you'll find a lot of hotels in the vicinity of the conference venue.


Public Transport

Public transport in Regensburg (RVV) is done by busses. All busses have a stop at Regensburg main station.
The price for a single ticket is 2.40 (ticket vending machines) resp. 2.80 (in the bus).
You can buy ticket strips (10 strips, 2 strips per ride) at 9.30 (ticket vending machines) resp. 10.30 (in the bus).
A day pass is valid starting at 09:00 for two persons and costs 5.00.

Please note: For the conference dinner we will provide a bus shuttle service
from the conference venue to the dinner location, and from the dinner location to the hotels.
 
In case of questions, please send an email to:  modelica2019@modelica.org
