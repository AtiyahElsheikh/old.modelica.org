72nd Design Meeting


Linköping University, Linköping, Sweden

Aug 31, 2011 10:00 AM

Sep 02, 2011 04:00 PM

The next Modelica Design meeting will take place in Linköping, Sweden, Aug 31 - Sep 2, at Linköping University Linköping University Map

For questions regarding administrative matters, travel, accommodation, please contact " asa.karrman[at]liu.se ", phone +46 13 281454

 

A block of 15 Rooms has been reserved at the nearby hotel Scandic West within 20 min walking distance of the meeting place. Please make your reservation soon. Call +46-13-495 50 00 or "linkoping [at] scandichotels.com" and give the booking code Modelica. to get the Special price of 871 SEK per night. (1 Euro = approx 9 SEK)


In the evening of August 31 at 18.00 there will be a guided tour and dinner at the AirCraft Museum in Linköping, One of Europe’s biggest! flygvapenmuseum Please email your participation to asa.karrman[at]liu.se and indicate whether you would like chicken or salmon for dinner "asa.karrman[at]liu.se".
