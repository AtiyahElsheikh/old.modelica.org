Travel and Accommodation Info

Meeting site

Politecnico di Milano, sede di Cremona.
Via Sesto, 41
26100 Cremona - Italy

The site is just at the outskirts of the town; it can be reached from the train station with a 20-minutes walk, or with a 5-minutes taxi ride.

Maps are provided for your convenience, see sidebar.

Getting to Cremona from abroad

By plane

Milano Malpensa Airport

Take the bus to Milano Centrale train station (starts every 20 min, approx. 50 min run, may take much longer during rush time), or the Malpensa Express train to Milano Cadorna  (starts every 30 min, 45 min run) and then the M2 underground to Milano Centrale. Trains to Cremona take approx. 1 hr (see below).

Milano Linate Airport

More confortable than Malpensa. Take the bus to Milano Centrale train station (starts every 20 min, approx. 25 min run). Trains to Cremona take approx. 1 hr (see below).

Bergamo - Orio al Serio Airport

Take the bus to Bergamo train station (approx 20 min. run). Trains to Cremona take approx. 1.30 hrs (see below).

Bologna Airport

Take the bus to Bologna Centrale train station (approx 30 min run). Trains to Cremona take approx. 2 hrs (see below).

By train

Cremona has a single train station, which is within walking distance from the city centre (10 min) and the university (20 min). Consult the Deutsche Bahn or Trenitalia websites for detailed timetables.

By car

Motorway A21, exit Cremona. For the city centre, follow the signs to "Centro città" (10 min). To get directly to the Politecnico site, take the ring road and follow the directions to "Milano - Pavia"; when you reach a big roundabout with directions to Milano on your right, turn left and follow the signs to "Politecnico". Please take notes that streets around Piazza del Duomo are closed to the traffic; finding a parking in the city centre can be difficult, and most of the parkings require a fee. Free parking spaces not far from the centre can usually be found along Viale Trento e Trieste (see Map).

Moving around in town

Depending on the participant's number and hotel locations, a shuttle bus and/or some private cars will be available. The distances are moderate, so it is also possible to walk, or to take taxis without too much expense.
